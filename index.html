About

I am a passionate and motivated Industry PhD (iPhD) candidate working to improve the agricultural industry by developing peptides for crop protection against fungal pathogens.

Research

Overview

My current research investigates whether natural and/or synthetic peptides can be used alone or synergistically with commercial fungicides to reduce the amount of pesticide applied on crops. This research uses a canola-blackleg model pathological system to investigate the application of peptides on plants for the treatment of fungal disease.

Outcomes

  • Work with multiple partners including farmers, industry and academics to understand the challenges faced
  • Identify natural and synthetic peptides with antifungal properties
  • Develop the underpinning science in order for new, biological products to be formulated and marketed
